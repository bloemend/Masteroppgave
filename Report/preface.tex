This study was performed as a master's thesis on behalf of the Department of Telematics at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology. This report is the final result of this master's thesis and is worth 30 ECTS points. The study was conducted between January and June 2013. The project description was outlined in cooperation with our project supervisor Sjur Usken from Aarbakke Innovation AS.

We would like to thank Sjur Usken who has guided us throughout our project, and contributed with helpful ideas, feedback and support. We would also like to thank our project professor Norvald Stol for his helpful feedback, and insightful and motivating comments. We would like to thank Sigurd Albrektsen for helping us put together the box. We thank the Village Telco community that have answered our questions and helped us along the way. Finally, we would like to thank everyone who helped us proof-read our report. \\
